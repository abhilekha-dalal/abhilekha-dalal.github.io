Abhilekha Dalal, Ph.D., is a Research Assistant Professor at Kansas State University. Her research focuses on Explainable AI, Deep Learning, and Semantic Knowledge Graphs, with applications in biomedical informatics, educational QA, and model transparency. She has led interdisciplinary collaborations and has published 8+ peer-reviewed papers. She also serves as reviewer and program committee member for conferences such as AAAI, ECAI, and ISWC.

Research Interests: Explainable AI, Deep Learning, Knowledge Graphs, Neuro-symbolic Learning, Semantics, Trustworthy AI
